# Chart Selection Justification

Sales Trend:
A line chart was used to visualize sales changes over time.

Regional Performance:
A bar chart was used to compare sales across regions.

Category Profitability:
A stacked bar chart was used to compare profitability across categories.

Customer Segment Analysis:
A bar chart was used to compare customer segments.

Shipping Performance:
A bar chart was used to compare average delivery days.

Filter Interaction:
A bar chart was used to demonstrate dashboard interactivity.
